my Firm...

When you have been injured because of someone else’s wrongdoing or while on the job, it can be a terrifying and anxious time. I have helped countless numbers of people who have been injured, representing adults and children in personal injury cases, the families of wrongful death victims, and employees injured in work related accidents. AboutMichelle E. Radice, Esq.

Michelle is a St. Louis native. She graduated from Marquette University in 2007 with a BA in English and History. She then returned to St. Louis and attended St. Louis University School of Law, graduating in 2010. Upon graduation, she worked for a defense firm, defending insurance companies who had workers’ compensation claims brought against them. After gaining some valuable experience and insight as to how cases are defended, she began representing individuals who had been injured. Michelle is licensed to practice law in both Missouri and Illinois, as well as the Eastern and Western Districts of Missouri.

Personal Injury

I represent individuals who have been injured because of someone else’s negligence. The results of an injury can be both financially and emotionally devastating, and I work to get my clients the compensation they deserve. This includes compensation for medical bills, lost wages, and pain and suffering.

Cases I handle include:
  • • automobile and trucking accidents
  • • pedestrians struck by vehicles
  • • bikers struck by vehicles
  • • slip and falls
  • • dog bites
  • • medical malpractice
  • • wrongful death
  • • products liability

Workers' Compensation

I represent employees who have been injured while on the job. If you are hurt on the job, even if it was not caused by anyone’s fault, you are entitled to compensation. I work to ensure that you get the necessary and appropriate medical treatment, that your medical treatment is paid for by the employer’s workers’ compensation insurance carrier, that you are paid for any missed time from work, and that you receive compensation for any permanent disability you suffer as a result of the work related incident.
